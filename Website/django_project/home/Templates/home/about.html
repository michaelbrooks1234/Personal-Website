    My name is Michael Brooks and I am a self taught developer working to find a job in software engineering.
    I program in c++, python, kotlin, and with frameworks such as django and Qt.

    Early Life - Current Day

    In my early life I was about the same as any other boy growing up. Making my way through school, playing with toys,
    and living a care free life. Nothing too characteristic of who I am now surfaced during this time.
    Later in life I was exposed to technology in the form of a Samsung tablet that I got for christmas.
    Playing games and watching videos on the tablet was very entertaining so I spent most of my time using it.
    This sparked my interest in technology and eventually I got into computer games.
    This is also the start of my interest of all things computers. From the hardware to the software, its all interesting
    Then as time went on I had come to the decision that software engineering would be the right career for me.
    So I got to work, first learning python just to start somewhere as the beginning was a very confusing journey.
    Then I learned about competative programming and how it could build your problem solving skills. Thats when I learned c++.
    Although it sounds like not much has happened, I spent a lot of time in the GUI and competitive programming phase.
    I did some work with GUIs and now I plan to move on to mobile app development and make some personal projects.

    The future

    Now as I hone my problem solving skills and build up a portfolio I plan on getting a job as some sort of developer.
    The short term plan is to get a job, but I want to make a career out of this. So that's the plan.
